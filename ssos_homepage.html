About SSOS

Your WHS compliance and safety experts in Australia

Helping businesses achieve compliance and workplace safety with ease

At Solving Safety On Site (SSOS), we are dedicated to helping businesses meet Australian Work Health & Safety (WHS) standards with expert guidance and practical solutions. With years of experience in SWMS writing, ISO certification, and safety compliance, we streamline the process to enhance workplace safety and efficiency.

We offer site safety audit services to identify potential hazards and implement effective risk controls. Our experienced team of safety management consultants ensure that your business remains compliant, reducing risks and creating a safer work environment.
